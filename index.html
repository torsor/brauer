Details: The 12th Brauer group meeting will take place June 8-12, 2015, with participants arriving on Sunday, June 7th, at the Pingree Park Mountain Branch Campus of Colorado State University. The conference will begin on Monday, June 8th, and the talks will end on Friday, June 12th, by noon. Participants are welcome to leave Friday June 12th or stay one additional day and depart on Saturday, June 13th.

Registration: If you are interested in attending and/or partipating by giving a talk, please fill out a registration form by using the link below. Some support will be available, although the exact amount is not yet determined. Funding priority will be given to graduate student participants and early career researchers.

Registration Form

Organizers: Eric Brussel, Kelly McKinnie and Danny Krashen

Facilities: The conference center meeting room will have projection equipment for laptop computers available, as well as chalkboards. Participants are welcome to use either the projectors or chalkboards for their talks.

Reimbursements: To reimburse your travel expenses, please bring to the conference any original receipts you have. If you are not a US citizen or permanent resident, we will also need a copy of your visa, I-94, and the photo page of your passport, along with a copy of your ESTA receipt.

If you bring a copy of your visa, I-94, passport and ESTA receipt with you to the conference, it will greatly help us to facilitate reimbursements! If this is not possible, you can send in your receipts and copies of these documents by mail after the conference.

Carpooling: For those of you who are interested in sharing rides from the Denver Airport or other locations to Pingree, please feel free to use the link below.

Brauer group at Pingree Park Carpool Site

If you are renting a car, you can register on the site as a driver by choosing 'add a new carpool,' and if you are looking for a ride, you can choose to 'join a carpool.'

Directions: The drive from Denver International Airport (DIA) to Pingree takes roughly 3 hours (about 1 hour to Fort Collins, then 2 more hours to Pingree). You can find maps and directions below!
